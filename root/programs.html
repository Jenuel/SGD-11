Featured Programs

Metro Manila Flood Management Project

The Metro Manila Flood Management Project, a joint effort between the Department of Public Works and Highways and Metro Manila Development Authority, aims to enhance flood management in selected areas of Metro Manila.

LEARN MORE

Toxic Substances and Hazardous Waste Management

The DENR-EMB program, in line with RA 6969, aims to manage industrial chemicals and hazardous wastes throughout their life cycle, from importation to disposal. This program aims to achieve environmentally sound management, reduce release to air, water, and soil, and minimize adverse impacts on human health and the environment.

LEARN MORE

Resettlement Program

The National Housing Authority (NHA) is responsible for housing production through the Resettlement Program (RP), which involves acquiring and developing large tracts of land to create serviced lots and housing units for families displaced from government infrastructure projects and danger areas.
